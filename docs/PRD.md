# Campus Companion

## Vision

Campus Companion is a centralized student productivity platform designed to simplify college life by bringing together academic resources, announcements, assignments, attendance, timetables, student support, and community interactions into a single dashboard.

Instead of replacing existing systems like WhatsApp or the college Resource Hub, Campus Companion complements them by organizing scattered information into a structured and student-friendly experience.

---

## Problem Statement

Students rely on multiple disconnected platforms such as WhatsApp groups, Resource Hub, Google Drive, classroom announcements, and personal notes to manage their academics.

This often results in:

- Missed announcements
- Forgotten assignments
- Difficulty finding study resources
- Poor attendance tracking
- Repeated questions to seniors and mentors
- Scattered academic information

Campus Companion aims to solve these problems through one unified platform.
